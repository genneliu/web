El Paso, Texas

Where the Wild West meets Mexico and Texas

    Fun Facts About El Paso, Texas:

  • El Paso is one of the top 10 safest cities in the US
  • About 68% of the population is bilingual
  • There are 6 border crossings into Mexico from the city
  • El Paso is the only city in Texas to operate on Mountain Time
  • The only jail Billy the Kid successfully broke into is in El Paso
